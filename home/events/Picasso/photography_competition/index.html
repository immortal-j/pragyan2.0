Picasso "Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is." If you are the one who has got immense passion towards photography and is avidly waiting for a platform to express your skills, Wait no more! Picasso is a theme based photography event in which individual will showcase their creativity using Camera. You can capture your picture either by DSLR or Mobile. Use this event as opportunity.
