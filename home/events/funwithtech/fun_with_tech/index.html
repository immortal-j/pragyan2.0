IT department invites you to take part in “FUN WITH TECH”. This event is biggest coding event in RCOEM and this year it gets even bigger under colosseum 12.0.

Everyone is welcome to test their skills! The event is divided into 3 rounds wherein you must qualify each round to move to the next.

Round 1 is general Aptitude round testing your reasoning thinking and critical thinking under pressure.

Round 2 is debugging round wherein you have to find bugs and errors in the code to test your understanding of given code.

Round 3 is programming round where you will be given with 5-6 problems with various difficulties to test your logical thinking and problem solving ability. This round can be solved with any programming language. Lastly this event hones your competitive programming abilities which will be crucial in placements! And we have exciting cash prizes for you.

So mark your calendar , make sure to fill out the registration form to book your spot and gear up the programmer within you.

Start date of the event : 19th January 2022

  • The event will be conducted in 3 rounds in online mode. 

  • Round 1: Aptitude Round. 

  • Round 2: Debugging Round. 

  • Round 3: Coding Round. 

  • Individual participation. 

  • Entry fee: Rs. 100 /-. 

  • Platform: Google forms and Codeforces

  • Rounds:

    1. Round 1:

      Aptitude Round – 20 questions

      Time limit – 25 min

      Total points – 20

    2. Round 2:

      Debugging Round – 5 questions

      Time limit – 20 min

      Total points – 20

    3. Round 3:

      Coding Round – 5 questions

      Time limit – 2 hrs

      Total points: Relative to final standings

  • Participants should join the google meet prior 10 minutes to the scheduled time. 

 

1) Every participant will get E-certificates.

2) There will be Exciting Prizes for the winners.

3) The event will be held on 19th,20th and 21st January 2022

4) Results will be declared on 22nd January 2022.
