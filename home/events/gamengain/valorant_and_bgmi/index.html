"Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence win championships."

Get ready with your armor as we have got a battlefield set for you to show your BGMI skills and Valorant Championship.

A one-of-a-kind, the biggest BGMI tournament and Valorant Championship is going to be held by STC for you to show off all your skills and win a prize poll.

Come and challenge the best, you have trained for it!! All contestants will get to enjoy the fierce match by sitting at their home itself...

  • There will be a set of challenges from a range of categories like Cryptography, Web Security, Forensics, Steganography, Reverse Engineering, Binary Exploits, etc. 

  • The challenges can either be a collection of files that they can download and find the “Flag” or can be running as web-services to which the participants connect and exploit to retrieve the Flag.

  • Each category contains a variety of questions with different point values and levels of difficulty. The participants will be ranked based on their scores, and the time taken will be used as a tiebreaker.

  • VALORANT

  • A player is allowed to have only one (1) Valorant account active on their account.
  • Each team’s roster must consist of at least 5 players.
  • Each team’s roster will be allowed to have 5 main players and 1 substitute. Once the tournament starts, you cannot replace any player.
  • Substitute player may be swapped with a core team player between games but not between rounds.
  • A substitute player may only be added before the Tournament Start and not for the duration of a match.
  • This player cannot be part of any other team’s roster which is participating or has participated in the same tournament.
  • A player is allowed to represent only one (1) team’s roster in the same tournament.
  • All games and matches will be hosted and provisioned by STC Valorant official coordinators.
  • Before the match begins, both team captains must report for veto 15 mins prior to the match time.
  • STC Valorant coordinators reserves the right to join any match as an observer. No external member may join and observe any match without consent of teams and STC Valorant admins.
  • Team captains of each team will have to choose maps and sides.
