Hello Creative Friends.... For those of you who are imaginative, creative and of course like to make posters let's join the poster competition which is organized by Semicolon Tech club under the theme "Technological support for green revolution" . Send us your Posters and grab an opportunity to win exciting prizes...

  • This event will be conducted online.

  • Entry Fees : Rs. 50/-

  • Last date of registration: 18 January 2022.

  • You have to present your poster on google meet.    

  • Use of direct templates from Google is not allowed.

  • If found, there will be direct disqualification.

  • Judging Criteria:  Creative idea, Proper Presentation, Clarity of topic, Overall Impact.    

  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

  • There will be Exciting Prizes for the winners.
