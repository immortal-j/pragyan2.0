  • An escape room is a game in which a group of people convene in a room loaded with interconnected riddles. In order for the team to escape and win, these problems must be solved before the timer runs out.

  • In Colosseum 12.0, this game will be played online. The competing teams will be allowed access to these cryptic spaces, and they will have to escape one digital area to enter another. There will be five digital chambers to keep things exciting and intriguing. 

  • It's simply the most incredibly entertaining test of your wit and ability to escape! 

  • It's time to put your detective abilities to the test and join us in this ultimate deciphering challenge. 

  1. This event will contain one round in online mode.

Rules:-

  • Team Size: 2-4 members

  • Entry fee: Rs. 150 /-

  • Rounds: Online Finals

  • Judging Criteria: Task Completion and time limit

  • Deadline for Registration: 18-01-2022

  • Total 4 escape rooms.

  • Time limit: Maximum 60 min for the whole event. (Each room will have a specific time limit and any group that does not complete the task in the allotted time, they will be eliminated.)

  • After escaping each room a google form will prompt and only leader will fill the form.

  • Participants should join the google meet prior 15 minutes to the scheduled time

 

  • Every participant will get E-certificates.
  • There will be Exciting Prizes for the winners.
  • Event will be held on 21 January 2022
  • Results will be declared on 22 January 2022.
