IDEATHON :- Hello Techies..!!! Semicolon Tech club is here to provide a platform for all the students to exhibit their innovative ideas with an exciting event - "IDEATHON" - It all starts with an idea. Here the students can form a team( 3 to 4 members) and pitch their idea with a PowerPoint presentation (PPT). The best ideas pitched are going to get exciting cash prizes

Online

Rules:-

  • No. of team members: 3 - 4 members.

  • Rounds: Online submission of abstract (at the time of registration on google drive link) & Online presentation Domains are open. Teams can choose any domain as per their interest.

  • Deadline for Submission of Abstract: 10th January 2022 & Result announcement – 11th January 2022.

  • Online Presentation: 13th January 2022 & Result announcement – 15th January 2022

  • Submission Criteria: Each and every team has to submit their team details (like team name, team leader, team members name etc) . Each team has to submit the abstract of their ideas in the format given to them. Rename your file as “(TeamName_YourCollegeName)

 

FAQ's:

 

 1) How to submit Abstract pdf?

Upload pdf with filename Teamname_YourCollegeName on Google drive link provided in registration form

 

2)Can we choose any field or domain?

Yes

 

3) Is there any time limit for presentation?

Yes, 20 min for each group in which each group member is required to speak
