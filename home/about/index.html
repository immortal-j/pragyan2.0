About

One of the premier institutions for technical education in the country, Shri Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management , Nagpur , has strived to provide students with the best academic syllabi and even better academic exposure since its establishment in 1984.

Under this legacy , In 2003 the Semicolon Tech Club(STC) was established . STC has come a long way and proved immensely beneficial for the students.

The Club has always strived hard to provide a platform for the students to exhibit their Technical, Co-curricular and Management skills.

STC organizes a National Level Technical mega event “Colosseum”, every year.
